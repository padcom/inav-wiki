RTH sequence can control altitude in several different ways, controlled by **nav_rth_alt_mode** and **nav_rth_altitude** parameters:

## Maintain current altitude (NAV_RTH_NO_ALT)
nav_rth_alt_mode = 0
nav_rth_altitude is ignored
